What we do

We offer a platform for businesses for consumer loyalty, advertising, market place and entrepreneurial opportunities. We do things differently from the tranditional business promotion and rewards programs out there. We are the only consumption recycling platform for consumers and businesses where your consumption dollars are put back in to your pockets in the form of redeemable cash, whether you are business, consumer or investor, our platform is built to just make your spent dollars find you again. There is no upfront cost to implement the program. Stay tuned for more information on "how it works". We are launching soon.

Best of the class benefits

The consumer benefit program of type that the industry has never seen before. Our platform offers an innovative rewards program that the consumers and businesses get rewarded for just being part of the transaction. The consumption recycling model enables the spent dollars walk (if not run) back to you.

Platform support

Mobile applications, online portals and our partner programs allow you to have a seamless experience, while seeing the real cash rewards just for doing your day to day shopping through your neighborhood merchants. Platform supports merchants and consumers find each other.

Similarly, once they shop and experience the benefit, in 98% of the cases, they will be your loyal customer just for the value of our "Consumption recycling" program.
